About

OpenQ is a free and open source alternative frontend for Q Student Connection, a student information system.


For instructions on hosting OpenQ for yourself, as well as for using its API, see the instructions on the project's README.


This project is licensed under the GNU GPL v3.


Note: OpenQ is currently a work-in-progress and very incomplete.
